SERVICES

At SulahMitra, we offer a range of mediation and dispute resolution services designed to help you reach peaceful, constructive outcomes. Our approach is non-adversarial and does not involve legal proceedings—we provide a structured, supportive space for open communication, avoiding the stress, expense, and delays of litigation.

Property & Neighbor Disputes

We facilitate constructive conversations about property boundaries, shared facilities, noise complaints, and other issues that arise between neighbors or property stakeholders.

  • Boundary disagreements
  • Tenant and landlord conflicts
  • Shared maintenance responsibilities
  • Noise and disturbance issues

Family Mediation

Our mediators help family members work through difficult conversations about inheritance, care arrangements, family businesses, and other sensitive topics.

  • Inheritance and estate planning
  • Elder care arrangements
  • Family business transitions
  • Extended family conflicts

Community Mediation

We work with community groups, homeowner associations, and other organizations to address collective concerns and conflicts affecting multiple stakeholders.

  • Homeowners association disputes
  • Public space usage conflicts
  • Community resource allocation
  • Neighborhood improvement initiatives

Business & Workplace Mediation

We provide neutral facilitation for business partnerships, workplace conflicts, and other professional relationships that require resolution of differences.

  • Partnership disagreements
  • Workplace conflict resolution
  • Vendor and client disputes
  • Team communication challenges

Our Mediation Process

1. Initial Consultation

We begin with a confidential discussion to understand the nature of your dispute and determine if mediation is the right approach.

2. Preparation

We help all parties gather relevant information and prepare for productive discussion in a neutral environment.

3. Mediation Sessions

Our trained mediators facilitate structured conversations where all parties can express concerns and explore potential solutions.

4. Agreement & Resolution

When consensus is reached, we document the agreement and provide guidance on implementation and follow-up.

5. Follow-Up Support

We provide follow-up support to help ensure the successful implementation of agreements and address any questions that arise during the transition.
